Here is our Research Goal


Research Goal: 
To investigate whether the frequency of Twitter interactions among Congress members is influenced by their respective party affiliations.

Hypothesis: 
Congress members from the same political party engage more frequently with each other on Twitter than with members of different parties.


Here is our Hypothesis Testing Plan:

Hypothesis: Congress members from the same political party engage more frequently with each other on Twitter than with members of different parties
Statistical Test: Two-sample t-test comparing the frequency of Twitter interactions between members of the same party and members of different parties while adjusting for confounding variables such as represented state and the chamber.
